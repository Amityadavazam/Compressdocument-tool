1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Simple Explanation: Think of cookies as memory notes that websites leave on your browser to remember your preferences and activities.

1.1. How Cookies Work

  • Created by websites you visit
  • Stored in your web browser
  • Sent back to the website on subsequent visits
  • Enable website functionality and personalization

1.2. Cookie Lifespan

  • Session Cookies: Temporary, deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain until expired or deleted
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by the website you're visiting
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by other websites via the current site

1.3. Why We Use Cookies

  • Ensure website functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Understand how tools are used
  • Support free service through advertising

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize cookies based on their purpose and duration:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you such as setting your privacy preferences or using tools.

Always Active - Required for site operation

Analytics Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us know which pages are the most and least popular.

Currently Active - Helps us improve tools

Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other sites.

Currently Active - Supports free service

Preference Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

Currently Active - Remembers your settings

  • Accept essential cookies only
  • Customize cookie preferences
  • Learn more about each cookie type

5.2. Manage Preferences

You can update your cookie preferences at any time by:

  1. Clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer
  2. Revisiting this Cookie Policy page
  3. Using the control panel above

To withdraw your cookie consent:

6. Browser Controls

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

Google Chrome

  • Click ⋮ → Settings
  • Privacy and security
  • Cookies and site data
  • Adjust settings

Mozilla Firefox

  • Click ☰ → Options
  • Privacy & Security
  • Cookies and Site Data
  • Manage permissions

Apple Safari

  • Safari → Preferences
  • Privacy tab
  • Manage Website Data
  • Remove or block

Microsoft Edge

  • Click ⋯ → Settings
  • Cookies and permissions
  • Manage cookies
  • Block or allow

6.1. Browser-Specific Instructions
