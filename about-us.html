Our Mission & Vision

We're on a mission to democratize access to professional document processing tools

Accessibility First

We believe powerful tools should be accessible to everyone. That's why all our tools are 100% free and work directly in your browser - no downloads, no installations, no hidden costs.

Privacy by Design

Our Story

From a simple idea to a comprehensive document processing suite

2023

The Beginning

Founded by Amarish Yadav, Compress Document Pro started as a simple project to help people process documents without expensive software. The first tool - PDF compression - was an instant hit.

Early 2024

Expanding the Suite

We added 10+ new tools based on user feedback, including PDF unlocking, merging, splitting, and image conversion tools. Our user base grew to 10,000+ monthly users.

Mid 2024

Mobile Optimization

Recognizing the shift to mobile, we optimized all tools for mobile devices. Our responsive design made document processing accessible anywhere, anytime.

Present Day

19+ Tools & Growing

Today, we offer 19+ professional tools serving 50,000+ users worldwide. Our commitment remains unchanged: free, secure, and instant document processing for all.
