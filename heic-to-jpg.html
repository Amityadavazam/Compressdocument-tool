HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is Apple's modern image format used by iPhones since iOS 11. It offers better compression than JPEG while maintaining higher quality, but isn't widely supported outside Apple ecosystem.

Our converter preserves maximum quality during conversion. You can choose quality settings: Maximum (minimal loss), Balanced (optimal), or Optimized (smaller size). The visual difference is minimal at high quality settings.

We support conversion to JPG (most compatible), PNG (lossless with transparency), and WebP (modern efficient format). You can also convert to all formats simultaneously for maximum compatibility.
