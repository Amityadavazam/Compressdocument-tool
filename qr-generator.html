Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about QR codes and SEO

How can QR codes improve my website's SEO?

QR codes bridge offline and online worlds. They can drive targeted traffic from physical materials to your site, increase engagement time, and provide data for local SEO when linked to your Google Business Profile. This can improve your search rankings by signaling to search engines that your website is relevant and frequently visited.

What's the best format for printing QR codes?

For small items like business cards, a minimum size of 2 x 2 cm (0.8 x 0.8 in) is recommended. Always use high contrast colors (dark on light background) and test before mass printing. For larger prints like posters, ensure the QR code is at least 10 x 10 cm (4 x 4 in) for easy scanning from a distance.

Can I track how many times my QR code is scanned?

Yes, by using dynamic QR codes. They allow you to edit the destination URL and provide scan analytics like time, location, and device. Our generator creates static codes; for tracking, consider dedicated platforms like Bitly or QRCode Monkey that offer analytics with their dynamic QR codes.
